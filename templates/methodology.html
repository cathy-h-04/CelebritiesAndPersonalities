Our Three Key Metrics For Finding Your Perfect Celebrity Match


Metric One: MBTI

MBTI, which is short for the Myers Briggs Type Indicator, provides a 4-letter personality type based on varying traits. Individuals can either by (E)xtraverted or (I)ntroverted, i(N)tuitive or (S)ensing, (T)hinking or (F)eeling, and (J)udging or (P)erceiving.


Metric Two: Enneagram

Enneagram, similar to MBTI, categorizes users based on their personalities. The enneagram system assigns users to one of nine personality types, Types 1 through 9, based on their personal characteristics and tendencies.


Metric Three: Name Analysis

The final method of matching a user with a celebrity involves using information based on a user's name. We use three APIs, agify, nationalize, and genderize, to match users and celebrities based on age, nationality, and gender, respectively. These three APIs analyze names and predict the most likely age, gender, and nationality.
